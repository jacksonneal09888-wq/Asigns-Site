DTF Prints

Direct-to-Film (DTF) printing is a revolutionary technology that allows for vibrant, full-color designs to be transferred onto a wide range of fabrics. Whether you need custom t-shirts, hoodies, or other apparel, our DTF prints offer exceptional detail, durability, and a soft feel.

Vibrant Colors

Achieve stunning, true-to-life colors that pop on any garment.

High Durability

Our DTF prints are highly resistant to cracking and fading, ensuring long-lasting wear.

Versatile Application

Suitable for various fabric types, including cotton, polyester, blends, and more.

Soft Feel

Enjoy a lightweight and soft-hand feel, making your custom apparel comfortable to wear.
